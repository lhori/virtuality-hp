What Virtuality is About

To communicate with each other and facilitate development we enlisted the aid of several tools:

Discord

Discord was used to create discussion, provide links to things, a place to ask questions and provide us with a way to understand where people at and how the development was going. This is accomplished by having channels of related discussion as well as online meetings via voice chat.

Trello

Trello was used to organize our list of tasks and things to do in a kanban style in which allows us to see what is being done, what is done and what is left to be done. Using Trello to track things was helpful when dividing tasks as well as understanding what features are left to be implemented.

GitHub

GitHub was used as our file repository and source control system for the project. By using GitHub we were able to work on the same project, merge and fix things as well as outline any issues when merging one branch with another.

Office 365

Office 365 was used to complete and facilitate our documentation via Word, provided us a source for our bug fixes/tracking document using spreadsheet, and allowed us to share it with one another on OneDrive. It was helpful to have a place for cloud storage as well as having applications that do things that we needed (Like word or spreadsheet).

Teledyne VR Assembly & Inspection Sim

A reproduction of an experience in which we allow users to inspect an assembly process of a digital camera.

With this product we are aiming to lower the risk and costs of delivering faulty products to customers by enhancing the training process of inspections. We do this by providing a virtual space in which staff or related individuals can experience what it’s like to inspect a product.

Features

Vitrual Scenarios

The simulation provides users with custom scenarios.

Immersive Environment

The simulation provides a stylish and relatable 3D environment which is explored by the user.

Interactive Objects

The simulation provides users with the ability to examine tasks and interact with tools to aid them.

Tracking and Analytics

The simulation tracks a user’s progress through the tasks and at the end grades them on their performance.

Conestoga Memorial Hospital: Evacuation Training

A simulation of hazardous scenarios in which users can experience and train on what to do when emergency occurs at a hospital.

This product should train and educate people on the procedures of how to evacuate patients and control the situation at hand. We do this by providing a virtual situation in which staff or related individuals take the perspective of a hospital staff member in charge of patients and assign them things to do when an emergency occurs.
